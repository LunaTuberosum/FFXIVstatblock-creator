This character cannot be Stunned and markers they have generated cannot be removed. Be sure to inform players of this before beginning the encounter.

Against Adversity

Odin’s Max HP is 125 per Enemy they are facing. If Odin is facing more than 4 enemies they perform one additional Primary Action.

Master of the Dread Arm

Odin’s [Zantetsuken] deals +2D6 damage if Odin is adjacent to a [Gungnir], consuming the [Gungnir] and causing the Unworthy enfeeblement to the target, which has the following effects:

  • An Unworthy character incurs a -2 penalty on all checks.
  • An Unworthy character has its speed reduced by 2.
  • An Unworthy character cannot wield a [Shard of Gungnir].
  • Unworthy removes the Worthy enhancement.
  • Unworthy is removed when a character takes 10 or more damage from a single source, deals 10 or more damage in a single attack, or heals 10 or more HP in a single ability

Dreadful Tension

This character does not take a turn, instead at the end of the [Adventurer Step] it deals 5 damage to all enemies and causes the Unworthy enfeeblement, which has the following effects:

  • An Unworthy character incurs a -2 penalty on all checks.
  • An Unworthy character has its speed reduced by 2.
  • An Unworthy character cannot wield a [Shard of Gungnir].
  • Unworthy removes the Worthy enhancement.
  • Unworthy is removed when a character takes 10 or more damage from a single source, deals 10 or more damage in a single attack, or heals 10 or more HP in a single ability

Worthy Prize

When a character reduces a [Gungnir] to 0 HP they gain the Worthy enhancement.

  • An Worthy character gains +2 bonus on all checks.
  • An Worthy character has its speed increased by 2.
  • An Worthy character can use a held [Shard of Gungnir] as a [Instant].
  • Worthy removes the Unworthy enhancement.

Elder Arm

When this character is reduced to 0 HP it is not removed from the battlefield. Instead inform the players that “The Mighty Gungnir will be wielded by the Worthy”. If they interact with the defeated [Gungnir] using a Primary or Secondary Action they gain [Shard of Gungnir] and access to the following [Duty Actions]. After a [Duty Action] is used they lose [Shard of Gungnir].

  • Boon of Steel (Primary, Ranged 10) You divide 10 Damage Reduction among any number of allies that lasts until the start of the next [Adventurer Step].
  • Heaven Piercer (Primary, Ranged 10) You deal 6D6 + 6 Damage divided among any number of enemies.
  • To Valhalla (Primary, Ranged 10) You heal 4D6 + 4 Hit Points, divided among any number of allies.
